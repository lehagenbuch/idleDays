Idle Days on the Yann is a short story originally written by an early fantasy writer Lord Dunsany. It was first published in 1910 in Dunsany’s short story collection A Dreamer’s Tales. This web-comic adaptation was illustrated and programmed by Lucy Hagenbuch.
